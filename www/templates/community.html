Wellness Mall

The Wellness Mall at Union Village is a place where Villagers as well as the community can come to find the best in wellness. Wellness is much more than healthcare; its goal is to bring all aspects of being human into balance. Most Wellness Centers have one or two centers of excellence. By providing a “mall concept”, the Wellness Mall at Union Village can coordinate the finest care in numerous centers of excellence. Rental space is available, only to the finest providers, on a yearly, daily and/or hourly basis.
