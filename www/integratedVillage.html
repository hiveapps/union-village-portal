The Integrated Health Villages at UnionVillage has been created to be the standard bearer for the future of healthcare.

It is a place where the traditional providers (Inpatient Care, Outpatient Care, Physicians, Skilled Nursing, Home Health, Senior Living, and Wellness) have merged to establish a complete continuum of care.

The Integrated Health Village master concept enables these providers ("Village Partners") to align philosophically and collaborate on the delivery of comprehensive, quality healthcare to their patients ("Villagers") The Village Partners are selected for their clinical excellence and expertise, and they embrace the Mission, Vision and Values of the Integrated Health Village.

The Village Integration concept includes a suite of solutions, including the provision of a common leading edge infrastructures of shared medical technology and record management services. The guiding principle of this medical-based integratoin is to take next generation healthcare to a new level for both clinical outcomes and overall patient experience.

The "continuum of care" concept has long been a topic of discussion amongst medical experts, but integrated Health Villages has transitioned this concept to actual construction. The Integrated Health Village at Henderson, Vevada is indeed the future of healthcare, bringing medical innovation to this and other integrated campuses around the world where people can; work and play, heal and hope, live and love, and learn and grow.
