Franklin Park – Assisted Living

Franklin Park – Assisted Living

At Franklin Park, we pride ourselves on providing assisted living services when they’re needed. There are no cookie cutter plans here—everything is customized to the individual, with the ability to add or drop services at any time. Whether it’s medication management, helping with mobility, coordinating care with your physicians, or making sure you look your best at happy hour, our friendly, professional staff is here to help.

Franklin Park – Memory Care

Franklin Park – Memory Care

We believe in caring for the whole person and the whole family. Dementia is difficult for everyone—there's no question. There's also no question that you're doing everything you can to help the person you love. But what if someone could help the whole family, and relieve the inevitable stress that comes from constant memory care? Franklin Park can help your loved one find meaning and contentment in each new day, so you can enjoy every moment you have with them.



We offer person-centered dementia and Alzheimer's care to the Las Vegas community because we believe families dealing with dementia can still finding meaning and comfort. Our programs fit the person and the family, not the other way around. We start by pairing our positive, loving, and empathetic caregivers with your loved one to craft a life story. This story becomes the foundation upon which we tailor each day, because it helps our caregivers visualize the special moments that have shaped your loved one's life.
